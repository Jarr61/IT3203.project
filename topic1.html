Flexbox Basics

Flexbox is a one dimensional layout system that helps arrange rows and columns

When should I use Flexbox?

  • Navigation bars or menus
  • Small boxes that you want to have compiled in rows
  • When you want things to center easily

Main Flexbox Settings

SettingWhat it does
display: flex;This property is used to create a flex container
flex-directionMakes items go in a row or column, an example would be "row-reverse"
justify-contentPads and spaces your items: left, right, or center
align-itemsAligns your items up or down
gapAdds more space between items
flex-wrapSpecifies whether flexible items should wrap or not
flex-flowThis combines the two properties of flex-direction and flex-wrap
